About the Artist

I'm a young photographer who loves capturing the quiet moments that most people don't always notice. My work explores how natural light, real emotion, and the beauty in everyday places come together to tell a story.

Even though I'm still a sophomore in high school, I've spent years practicing and growing my skills. I've had the chance to work with people who appreciate honest, meaningful photos, and I'm always excited to learn more. My approach is simple: find the extraordinary in the ordinary.

Each photograph I take is an invitation to pause, look a little closer, and see the world from a different perspective â€” even if it's just for a moment.

I plan a career in aviation keeping photography as a hobby. I plan to take photos of the different cities and countries I visit focusing on urban photography. My other interests include Programming (mostly vibe coding), Mountain Biking (I race for NC NICA), Electrical Engineering and video games (Apex Legends, Rocket League, and some War Thunder).

My favorite saying is "Memento mori" (remember that you must die). This reminds us that death is a part of life and to enjoy the world you live in while you can.
